Formation of EESA

EMSA-Ethiopia is the first student-led pre-professional association in Ethiopia. Even though medical student-based organizations are not a new concept in the world, in Ethiopia there was no formally formed association that coordinated efforts of voluntary students to make effective social change both in Ethiopia and in the global health system. The initiator was Dr. Ergeba, who was learning abroad at the time. She was inspired after she attended a conference organized by medical students at her university. With Dr. Birhanu (a fourth-year medical student at AAU at the time), they organized students with similar goals in 2003 G.C. They tried to see the experience of similar associations around the world and managed to make the association a full member of the International Federation of Medical Students Association (IFMSA) with a yearly membership fee covered by Ethiopian doctors living abroad who supported the idea.
